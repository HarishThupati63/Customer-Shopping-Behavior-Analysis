Results & Key Insights

Revenue: Male customers bring in about twice the revenue of female customers.

Product Performance: Gloves, sandals, and boots are among the top-rated items.

Discounts: Highest discount usage is found in hats and sneakers.

Customer Segmentation: Loyal customers are the majority of the base.

Subscription Impact: Subscribers are a minority share, whereas non-subscribers drive most of the revenue.

Strategic Recommendations:

Encourage subscription growth through special offers and targeted marketing campaigns.
